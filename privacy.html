1. Introduction

At Azee Restaurant, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our restaurant, use our website, or interact with our services.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number)
  • Reservation and dining preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely, not stored)
  • Delivery addresses
  • Special dietary requirements

2.2 Automated Data Collection

When you visit our website:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Cookies (see Section 5)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process reservations and orders
  • Personalize your dining experience
  • Improve our services and menu offerings
  • Send promotional communications (with consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (delivery partners, booking systems)
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business transfers (if we undergo mergers/acquisitions)

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Enable online ordering functionality

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Security

We implement:

  • SSL encryption for online transactions
  • Secure storage protocols
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data

While we take reasonable measures, no electronic transmission is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at abdajees01@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. Children's menu preferences are only collected when dining with guardians.
